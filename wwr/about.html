About Us - Rafting Compas

Our mission is to provide exciting and safe rafting adventures, connecting people with nature and creating unforgettable memories on the river.

History

Founded in 1998 by a group of passionate river guides, Whitewater Adventures began with a single raft and a dream to share the thrill of the river. From our humble beginnings on the Colorado River, we've grown into a premier outfitter, guiding over 50,000 adventurers down wild rivers while maintaining our core commitment to safety, sustainability, and pure adrenaline-fueled fun.

Today we operate across multiple rivers with state-of-the-art equipment and certified guides who prioritize both safety and unforgettable experiences for all our guests.
